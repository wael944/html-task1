Club Instructions

To maintain a safe and enjoyable environment, please adhere to the following club rules:

  • Wear Protective Gear: All riders must wear a helmet and appropriate riding gear at all times.
  • Follow Trainer Instructions: Always listen to and follow the guidance of our professional trainers to ensure safety.
  • Respect Horses and Other Riders: Treat all horses and club members with respect and care. Do not approach horses without permission.
  • Maintain Cleanliness: Keep the stables, arena, and club areas clean and tidy. Dispose of trash properly and clean up after your horse.
